About InnovateX

International Conference on AI & Data Science 2025 (Innovatex-2025) is the second edition hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Panimalar Engineering College, in association with IEEE Madras Chapter, IEEE Computer Society Madras, and Computer Society of India-Chennai.

Innovatex-2025 aims to provide an outstanding opportunity for both academic and industrial communities to address challenges in **Data Science, AI, and Agent Technologies**. The conference will feature research articles from across the globe, distinguished keynote speakers, and tutorial sessions.




Our Mission

✔️ To create next-generation leaders through effective teaching methodologies and scientific innovation.

✔️ To transform lives through **emerging technology, novelty, and sustainability**.

✔️ To inculcate **human values and ethical principles** to cater to societal needs.

✔️ To foster a **strong research ecosystem**, bridging academia, industry, and R&D.

✔️ To **nurture incubation centers**, enabling structured entrepreneurship and start-ups.
